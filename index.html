Gallardo facts:

  • In Lamborghini’s 50 years of existence, it has built around 30,000 cars. Of these 30,000 cars, nearly half of them - half - were Gallardos.
  • 14,022 built throughout its production run
  • Named after a famous breed of fighting bull
  • On 25 November 2013, the last Gallardo was rolled off the production line
  • The Gallardo was replaced by the Huracán in 2014
  • The vehicle was designed by Luc Donckerwolke
